Central Swimming Pool

Rakhat Fitness Sports Club has some of the best swimming pools in Kazakhstan. (Olympic Pool) 50 × 21 m, consisting of eight lanes;
       25 × 21 m with seven tracks and one track with a false bottom (minimum depth 70 cm) In the summer, our pool becomes the largest open-air reservoir within the city: in early May, the dome is removed and a spacious recreation area with mountain views is formed. Next to the pool there are sun loungers and a summer bar with gazebos.

The sports club is located in the city center. The nearest metro station is located five minutes walk from the club. Also nearby is the Central Stadium, the Palace of Sports. Baluan Sholak, Kazakh State Circus, Theater. M. Auezov.

Our coaches are coaches of the highest category, champions and prize-winners of various international championships, participants of the Olympic Games. Their pupils are winners of status competitions, such as, for example, Dmitry Balandin, the first representative of Kazakhstan in history who won the Olympic gold.
